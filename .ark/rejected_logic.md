# ARK | REJECTED LOGIC & ALTERNATIVES

## **OVERVIEW**
This registry documents architectural paths, libraries, or logic patterns that were considered and subsequently **REJECTED**. It prevents the "circular error" of re-evaluating failed ideas without historical context.

## **REJECTED LOG TEMPLATE**
### [ARK_ID] | Title of the Rejected Concept
- **Alternative:** Description of the discarded solution.
- **Reason for Rejection:** Why it was deemed unsuitable (e.g., technical debt, scalability, violation of principles).
- **Context:** Original rationale before rejection.
- **Re-evaluation Trigger:** Conditions under which this path might be reconsidered.
